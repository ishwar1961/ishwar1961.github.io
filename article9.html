Simple Digital Products You Can Sell Online (2026)

Best Tools to Create & Sell Digital Products →

Digital products are the easiest way to earn money online. No shipping, no investment, and you can sell the same product unlimited times. Perfect for beginners who want passive income.

1. Canva Templates

You can create Pinterest templates, Instagram posts, YouTube thumbnails, resumes, or business flyers.

Best for: Beginners & creators.

2. Ebooks

Write simple 10–20 page ebooks on topics like fitness, cooking, digital marketing, or money earning.

3. Notion Templates

People love productivity tools. You can create planners, habit trackers, budget templates, etc.

Try These Digital Product Ideas →

4. Printable Worksheets

Such as study planners, budgeting sheets, task lists, and daily routines.

5. Stock Photos

Take photos with your phone and sell them on stock websites.

6. Online Mini Courses

Create short video lessons teaching one skill — editing, Canva, affiliate marketing, etc.

7. Digital Stickers

Thousands of people buy stickers for planners, notebooks, and digital journals.

Conclusion

Digital products are perfect for passive income. Start with one simple product, upload it, and promote using Pinterest for fast results.
