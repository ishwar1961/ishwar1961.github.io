Best Keyword Research Tips for Beginners (2026)

Use the Best Keyword Tools →

Keyword research helps you find topics people search for but with low competition. If you want your blog to grow fast in 2026, you must master simple keyword techniques.

1. Start With Long-Tail Keywords

Use phrases with 3–5 words like:

  • how to start affiliate marketing
  • best SEO tips for beginners
  • low competition niches 2026

2. Look at “People Also Ask” on Google

These questions are perfect topics because they rank easily and bring fast traffic.

3. Use Free Keyword Tools

  • Google Keyword Planner
  • Ubersuggest (free version)
  • KeywordTool.io
  • AnswerThePublic

4. Check Pinterest Trends

Pinterest trends show what people are searching for right now — perfect for blog ideas and quick traffic.

5. Analyze Low-Competition Blogs

Search your keyword → open small blogs → copy their topics and improve them.

6. Use “Alphabet Method”

Type your main keyword in Google, then add:

  • a
  • b
  • c

Example: “blogging tips a” → Google will show dozens of ideas.

7. Focus on Search Intent

Make sure the topic matches what users really want:

  • Informational (guides, how-to)
  • Commercial (reviews, comparisons)
  • Transactional (buy now)

Conclusion

Keyword research is simple when you follow a consistent method. Focus on low-competition keywords, answer real user questions, and use free tools to grow faster.
