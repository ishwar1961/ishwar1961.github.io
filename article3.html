Best Monetag Ad Formats for Earnings

1. In-Page Push

High CTR, beginner-friendly, and ideal for mobile users. Shows a non-intrusive notification-style ad.

2. Popunder Ads

Opens an ad in the background. Strong earnings but use carefully.

3. Floating Banner

A lightweight banner that sits at the bottom of the page. Good for clean UI and passive clicks.

4. SmartLink

Promote on Pinterest, WhatsApp, Facebook, YouTube or anywhere. No website required.
