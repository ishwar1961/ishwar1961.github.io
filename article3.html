How to Use Monetag to Earn Daily (Beginner Friendly Guide 2025)

Monetag is one of the easiest online earning platforms because you can earn money even if your visitors don’t buy anything. Whether you have a small blog, a Pinterest profile, or social traffic, Monetag helps you generate daily earnings.

This complete guide explains how Monetag works, best ad formats, earning tips, and how to set it up on your website — step-by-step.

What Is Monetag?

Monetag is an advertising network that pays publishers for traffic. Unlike traditional affiliate marketing, Monetag pays for user actions and impressions — not sales.

Why Monetag Is Perfect for Beginners

  • No approval required
  • Works even with low traffic
  • Earnings from mobile users
  • Multiple ad formats
  • Good for Pinterest + WhatsApp + Blogs
  • No selling required

Best Monetag Ad Formats for Daily Earnings

1. Popunder Ads

Popunder ads appear in a new browser tab. They generate high revenue because advertisers pay more for this format.

  • Best for desktop + mobile
  • Highest CPM
  • Good for organic traffic

2. In-Page Push

These ads look like push notifications but work on all browsers. They blend with the content and have high user engagement.

3. SmartLink (Highest beginner earnings)

SmartLink is a single link that automatically redirects visitors to the highest-paying offer based on their device, country, and behavior.

Perfect for:

  • Pinterest bio link
  • WhatsApp groups
  • Facebook posts
  • Landing pages
  • Banners inside articles

4. Floating Banner

This is a small banner that stays at the bottom of the screen. It is non-intrusive but gives stable income daily.

How to Add Monetag Ads to Your Website

You have already integrated Monetag on your website, but here’s the step-by-step process for others:

Step 1 — Sign Up

Create your account on Monetag.

Step 2 — Add Your Website (Optional)

If using SmartLink, you don’t need a website. But for Popunder and Push, add your site.

Step 3 — Generate ad codes

Select the formats you want.

Step 4 — Paste the codes into your website

You should place them before the closing </body> tag.

Where to Place Monetag Ads for Best Results

  • Inside articles (like this page)
  • Home page footer
  • Between paragraphs
  • Below images
  • Inside buttons (SmartLink)

How Much Can You Earn with Monetag?

Earnings depend on:

  • Traffic country
  • Traffic source
  • User behavior
  • Ad formats used

Typical CPM ranges:

  • $4–$12 for Tier 1 traffic
  • $2–$6 for Tier 2
  • $0.5–$2 for Tier 3

Tips to Increase Monetag Earnings

1. Use multiple formats together

Popunder + Floating Banner + SmartLink gives the best results.

2. Get more mobile traffic

Mobile users convert the most for Monetag.

3. Publish long articles

Longer content = more ad impressions.

4. Use Pinterest traffic

Pinterest gives thousands of impressions for free.

5. Add SmartLink to all CTA buttons

This gives extra revenue.

Conclusion

Monetag is one of the easiest ways for beginners to start earning online. With simple setup, multiple ad formats, and high CPM rates, you can generate daily income even with small traffic.

Use Popunder for high earnings, SmartLink for external traffic, and In-Page Push for engagement — the perfect combination for growing daily revenue.
